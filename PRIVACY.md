# Privacy

Apsenz Apple Music is designed to show only Apple Music activity in Discord Rich Presence.

## What it reads

The app/plugin reads Apple Music playback information such as:

- Song title
- Artist
- Album
- Playback progress
- Artwork when available

## What it does not use

Apsenz Apple Music does not intentionally use:

- YouTube playback
- Browser media sessions
- Video players
- Other apps playing media

If Apple Music is not detected, Rich Presence should be cleared instead of showing another app.

## Data collection

Apsenz Apple Music does not collect, sell, or upload personal data.
